Residue

Where? Where is my unflinching scrutiny when I need it? It’s like my ultimate weapon is gone when the time comes. Just as Karna in Kurukshetra.

Confession

God forgive me for wasting this opportunity
And deny the possibility.
Forgive me for being such a fool.
For being full of belief and opinions.
For lived as a mere pleasure seeker.
For I was ignorant and lived within the mind.
Peace and freedom is nowhere to be found

I have suffered and I have suffered enough
I can laugh and smile but it won’t last
For I have come to know the nature of it.
in silence, in the wild, and in dark only
I know that it is me
A fear based creature.
An un-efficient machine.

Now I will beg you sir
To bestow your grace upon me
Give me the power to destroy.
Give me the sincerity to see
Let me see, let me destroy.
Let me dance in my own destruction.

Now I become death
The destroyer of world.

Rock Solid Foundation

Peace, peace, peace!
A place where all things reside.
Where the poet gets his inspiration.
Where the warrior pick up his courage.

Peace, peace, peace!
I witness my burden slowly fell off.
The chain is cracking.
By the grace of the sun I can see.
And when the dark comes,
I know, whom shall I slain.

The monkey king is back.
