Berkah Dalem

The last three months were an absolute roller coaster — from the deepest, uncharted places where life feels so freakin’ real, to a shallow, artificial, fake, insincere drama man plays to fill the void. I’ve seen the truth behind man’s actions: one moved by desire, the other by necessity. The fancy word for the latter is compassion — a word so often misunderstood as a “good quality,” when in truth it’s simply… the truth. No more, no less.

This is when there’s no way it could be otherwise. In the words of Obi-Wan:

"I will do what I must"

Anyway… what a ride.
