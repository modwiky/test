Coffee and Hope

"Thy right is to work only, but never to its fruits; let not the fruit-of-action be thy motive, nor let thy attachment be to inaction"

…

Duh, it should comes at no surprise that the only thing that prevent me to enjoy a cup of coffee is my hope for all the goodness of it. Performance boost and increase focus. Tweaking the metabolism as they say.. What is hope anyway? is it just another bloat?

YES

Hope is just a progam installed on your machine that do nothing but slow you down. We use it as a mean to deal with something we don't understand. In my case, my hope for the goodness of coffee contribute nothing to what really happens. I mean, it is the nature of caffein that makes the magic happen. You see, hope do nothing there so why hope in the firstplace?

Now, how all this come to play? well this is the problem of belief and how human has been so condition to operate within this framework. Someone told you long ago to hope for a better tomorow and you just belief it because it sounds right or it helps you to go through the hardship of life.. you accept it, whisper it on your prayer, integrate it to your system and becomes the basis of your action. Everybody doing it anyway so why not?

Thus instead of go to the core of the problem, figuring out what the hell is going on, questioning the nature of it, we use the easy way to simply hope for the magic to happen. But alas, the easy way is never the way, nor is the hard way is the way. the only way is the way of truth.
